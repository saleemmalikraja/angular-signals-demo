Discover Angular Signals

Discover *Angular Signals* - a new way to handle data flow in your apps! ⚡️ Let's explore its strengths/weaknesses and its impact on our projects. 🧐🔍

🤔 Signals address:
🚫 Monkey-patching async/await
🐌 Inefficient change detection
🔮 Zone.js errors
🐘 Overhead from Zone.js

🏗️ Solutions:
✅ Native async/await support
✅ No more Zone.js & monkey patching
✅ Reduced bundle size
✅ Targeted updates for better performance

📈 Strengths:
✅ Automatic dependency tracking
✅ Glitch-free execution
✅ Dynamic dependency tracking
✅ Equality semantics & value versioning
✅ Lifecycle management & garbage collection

📉 Weaknesses:
🚩 Complexity
🚩 Performance overhead
🚩 Unspecified execution timing
🚩 Learning curve
